? make the patten optional
| or
\w word
\W not a word
\s space
\S not a space
\d digit
\D not a digit
\b word boundary
\B not a word boundary
^ start of line
$ end of line
\A start of string
\Z end of string
\d{3} number of times the digit repeat
\d{2,5} number of times the digit repeat (2) is min and (5) is max
\d{2,} number of times the digit repeat (2) is min and (infinity) is max
\d* number of times the digit repeat (0) is min and (infinity) is max
\d+ number of times the digit repeat (1) is min and (infinity) is max
(html|body) match either html or body
<h1>(.*?)<\/h1> match the content between <h1> and </h1>.

Character classes.
[\d\w] matching any digits or letters.
[\d\w]+ matching any number of digits or letters.
[0-5] matching range of digits.
[a-z]+ matching range of letters.
[A-Z]+ matching range of uppercase letters.
[a-zA-Z\/\s]+ matching range of letters and symbols.
[^\s]+ everything except spaces.
(\w{3}-) matching three letters followed by a hyphen.
(\w{3}-\d{3}-) .*?\1 matching three letters followed by a hyphen and three
digits followed by the same hyphen.

Positive lookahead and lookbehind.
\w(?=\s)
>(?!\s) look for the right arrow not followed by a space.
^(?!.*html).*?$ match every line except HTML.

Flags | Modifiers
i case insensitive
g global
m multi line
.*?\.\w+ match any line ending with a period followed by one or more letters.
